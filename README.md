# 🌱 My AI Career Journey

Welcome.

This repository is my personal timeline documenting the transition from farm owner and operational thinker to AI Operations professional.

Rather than only documenting projects, this repository documents the moments that changed me.

Every milestone is called a **Seed**.

Just like on a farm, every harvest begins with a single seed.

This is where I plant mine.

---

## 🌱 What is a Seed?

A Seed is a meaningful moment in my AI journey.

Some Seeds celebrate accomplishments.

Some document failures.

Some capture lessons that changed the way I think.

Together, they tell the story of my growth.

---

## 🌱 My Philosophy

I don't believe you need a computer science degree to begin learning AI.

I believe you need curiosity, consistency, and real problems to solve.

Every Seed represents another step forward.
